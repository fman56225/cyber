Aim:Vulnerability Analysis using Nikto

Log in to kali Linux and open Terminal

nikto -h http://testphp.vulnweb.com/listproducts.php?cat=1

Note a vulnerability number, for example 23654, and open a web browser

Type the URL https://cve.mitre.org/ in the browser to open the common Vulnerabilities 
and Exposures websites

 Click on Search CVE List and type your vulnerability number in the text box, as shown in 
figure and press enter.
